Dr. Strangehop

American Extra Pale - XPA

6.5%
  • Description
  • History

We use an ultra-clean malt bill so that nothing interferes with the fruity and pungent hop aromas and flavors. The Dr. capitalizes on first wort hopping and liberal flavor and aroma hop additions, as well as extensive dry hopping. The unique blend of powerful American hops delivers a prominent juicy flavor, without astringent bitterness. Mango, tangerine, apricot and even the classic orange drink “Tang” are a few flavors and aromas that come to mind when gulping down the XPA.
Orange/Light Amber

Most beer fans are already familiar with the origin of hoppy India Pale Ales; England, India, Hoppy. With the same hop-centric focus as American IPA’s, Dr. Strangehop pushes the American Pale Ale in a tasty direction with abundant, yet clean, tropical hop flavors. If you’re not yet a XPA fan, stop worrying and learn to love the hops.
